Our Story

When covid first started the only thing that was open were the lakes, we found ourselves going boating 4-5 times every week. Wake surfing became our passion and we found ourselves wanting to surf all day the only problem was the daylight. We knew there had to be a way to stay out late and surf longer. And that is how surfmongers was born.

Our Goal

Our mission is to bring a new twist to surfing. We know that on the lake that is where memories are made. We know that many people have tried night surfing but it can become very dangerous when they fall in the pitch black on the lake. We want to make night surfing safe and more enjoyable.

How it works

What we have created here is something new and unique. We started with many different protoypes trying to decide what the best option would be to make accessories for your life jacket as well as for your surfboard. So after months of testing this is what we came up with. A light vest that works like a harness and a light kit that can attach to any surf board. This is how they work.

Light Vest

This accessory works on any life jacket that you own. It comes in 2 sizes S/M and L/XL. It wears like any normal vest, You simply put it on top of the life jacket.

Surfboard Lights

This accessory allows you to put a light kit on any surfboard you already own. You simply peel off the adhesive on the attachable lights and apply them to your Surfboard.

Supplies Required

All you need is a surfboard, Life jacket, and some AA batteries for both the vest and the surfboard.

Safety Tips

Make sure that all battery containers are closed tightly and make sure you let the surboard kit adhesive dry on the board for at least 24 Hours.
